The Journey: From Lovedale to the Podium

In March 2025, I made a life-defining choice: I left The Lawrence School, Lovedale, one of India's premier boarding schools, to dedicate my life to the wind and the ocean.

Trading the classroom for a full-time training sabbatical has allowed me to engineer a breakthrough in my performance. Through elite fitness, strict nutrition, and relentless hours on the water, I have moved from the middle of the fleet to a National Bronze Medalist in just 12 months.
